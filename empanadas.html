Empanadas

Description

Español: Los empanadas son una comida de españa. Es una bola de masa rellena.

English: Empanadas is a dish from spain. It is a filled doughbag

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 10g wheat flour
  • 0.02TS baking powder
  • 6.25g butter
  • 0.03 eggs
  • 0.13FS water
  • 0.03g salt

For the filling

  • 0.06 onions
  • 0.02 peppers
  • 0.06FS sunflower oil
  • 15.63g minced meat
  • 0.09FS tomatomark
  • 0.03g salt and pepper
  • 0.03g cayenne pepper
  • 0.03TS pepperpowder (edelsweet)

Steps to make empanadas

  1. Mix flour with baking powder
  2. Kneed the eggs with water and salt and then let it rest in foil for 60 minutes
  3. Cut the onions and peppers into tiny cubes and let them heat up in a pan with oil
  4. Let it cool down in a bowl. Then mix it with the cubed vegetables eggs salt pepper and cayennepepper
  5. Roll the dough on a baking plate and then make circles in the dough (with a radius of 5cm) and then put 1 full spoon in the middle of the circle
  6. Fold the circles to a half moon and let it bake for about 15 minutes in the oven in 180 C°
